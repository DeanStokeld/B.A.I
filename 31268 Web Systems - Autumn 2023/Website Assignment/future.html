Future

Career Goals

In the future I would hope to start a career in Data Science field, whether that's Data Analytics, Data Engineering or Data Science. However I have a keen interest in Machine Learning and A.I. so a position in Data Science would be ideal.

I have these aspirations because I very much enjoy working with data and building ML models.

Skill Goals

At the moment I have foundational skills in Python and some of its Data Science-related libraries, such as the ones listed in the Past page and more.

So an aspiration of mine would be to increase my knowledge and skills in Python as well as becoming more familiar with its libraries. In addition to this, I aim to also learn the programming language R, as I've heard this is still used in the field however I believe Data Scientists are slowly transitioning away from R and replacing it with Python.
